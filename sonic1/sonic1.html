GAME BASICS

Sonic the hedgehog is a platforming game where you can build lots of speed by running and spinning. There are different routes you can pick to reach the goal of the level, with checkpoints, time limit, powerups and rings you can pick up that gives you advantages like invulnerability, lives and continues.

There are 6 zones in total, each divided in 3 acts, and 1 final zone at the end of the game. You will fight robotnik as a boss at the end of act 3 in every zone, and you will encounter him for the last time at the final zone.

There are checkpoints throughout the acts. To get the checkpoint you have to find a lamp post and cross it. The lamp post will become red.

There is a time limit of 10 minutes. When you cross a checkpoint, your current time will be saved so if you die, you

will respawn at the last checkpoint and the timer will reset to the time you had when you crossed the checkpoint. If you crossed the checkpoint within a few seconds before timing out and you time out, the timer will reset to 0 so you don’t softlock.

Sonic can interact with the enviroment through the stages, there are walls that you can break, secret paths, switches, platforms that can be activated, platforms that will fall, appearing platforms, rotating platforms, and other platforms that you can interact with.

There are enemies all over the stages. Most of them can be killed by bouncing on them while you are a ball. All enemies take 1 hit. Some others you can’t land on, but you can roll past them instead. Enemies respawn if you go far enough from them.

There is a boss at the end of

every act 3. Bosses take 8 hits and you can bounce on it only 3 times in a row, because every time you bounce, sonic's sprite moves down a little bit so after 3 hits you are under the boss’s hitbox so you clip down and might take a hit. I recommend to bounce two times to prevent being hit.

There are multiple ways to die. The main one is If you have 0 rings and you get hit by an enemy or a dangerous obstacle like spikes or else, after the invulnerability stops.

The insta deaths that don't depend on rings are landing on spikes twice, getting crushed by platforms, falling out of the screen, drowning or running out of time.

You can look up or down if you press up or down button. This is very useful to see enemies or platforms above you, or if there's no screen under you. Not so many people seem to know about this feature.

There are two endings in this game. To get the good ending you must collect 6 chaos emeralds hidden in bonus levels. A big ring will appear after crossing the goal with at least 50 rings in every act 1 and 2. You jump inside it to enter the bonus level.

The bonus level is a rotating maze with different obstacles and a chaos emerald protected behind prisms in the middle of the level. You must break these prisms standing on them, while controlling sonic as a ball, using the scenario, gravity and obstacles to your advantage. Once you pick the emerald, you will exit the level.

TIPS!: Pressing any direction while standing on a wall makes sonic spin faster. The next time you press jump you will get propulsed very fast and far, so the best way to control sonic through the bonus level is to jump and press directions after jumping.

There are only 1 chaos emerald per bonus level, and 6 chaos emeralds in total. You have 12 attempts to pick all of them. If you fail and exit the labyrinth without the emerald, the next time you enter the bonus level will be the next emerald you are missing. MOMENTUM

Sonic can run and spin. Your speed is very limited when you run, but when you spin you can reach faster speeds (it still has a limit, but much higher). You spin if you press down after building some speed.

You build speed on slopes and falls, or from bouncing on a spring. Pressing any direction slows you down, and switching platforms makes sonic get out of the spin, so the best way to spin is pressing only down and holding the button.

You can build speed faster if you jump and run afterwards. If there’s little space, jump first to the opposite side you want to run, turn around and jump again to reach max running speed.

The way momentum works in this game is that you will get

boosted up in a direction after jumping depending on where you are standing and your speed. First off, we will explain momentum when you have built a lot of speed and then jump:

If you jump while going uphill with a lot of speed, you will get boosted upwards in a diagonal.

f you jump on flat ground you will get boosted up horizontally.

If you jump while going downhill you will get boosted downwards

Sonic always jumps higher on a slope. If you stand still or you haven’t built much speed then you will get pushed outwards depending on where on a slope you are standing.

As you can see you will get pushed away from the slope, making this very difficult to climb up.

It’s very important to understand this concept to make it back up after running down accidentally. Don’t jump on the slope to get back up, instead, use the jump and run mechanic for building up speed in small spaces that we just learned, I will show you how to do it in this case: Stand at the bottom of the slope where it’s flatter, boost a jump to the opposite direction, and jump again to the direction you want to go. That second jump also has to be on the flat side of the slope.

If you jump before a ramp you will boost your speed faster. To keep building up speed you can hold the down button and your speed will increase more and more and more.

There’s a little trick you can perform if you are in the middle of a loop and press jump and down. You will gain max speed immediately, but it’s a little bit tricky to perform.

Landing on enemies or objects while holding jump makes you bounce higher. Falling from a longer distance will make your bounce even higher.

In the chaos emerald levels, if you keep pressing any direction will make sonic start spinning faster and faster. If you press jump afterwards you will jump faster and longer distances.

TIPS

Even if this game is about speed, playing slow is the best way to avoid landing on random enemies. Play safe at the beginning of the level until you see rings.

You can leave some rings behind in case you get hit so you can quickly come back and pick them up if you lost all your rings after getting hit.

Usually the very bottom routes are the hardest. I will show you the easiest routes in this guide in PLAYTHROUGH.

As we learned, jumping and running is the best way to gain speed very fast and get you out of compromised spots.

To get to different platforms, always jump, because even though you can walk towards them most of the time, the walk is slow and slippery so it can

make you fall out of the platform with the momentum. Jumping gives you more control and you will stand in place once you land.

Neutral jumping to get to platforms above you.

Neutral jump to wait and guarantee landing on another platform if you are not so sure about it.

You can respawn or reset platforms if you leave them offscreen and come back.

After bouncing on a spring you won't be in ball form anymore, so you won't kill enemies.

Never get close to a wall after jumping on a spring or you might get hit by enemies at the edge of the platform, since as we said, you won't be in ball form anymore.

Try to pick up the emeralds in the early levels since it’s easier to get to the goal with 50 rings.

The best way to get to the labyrinth is pressing left before the goal to reduce your speed. You have time until the music changes to the beating the stage music so if you ran offscreen, you can still come back to pick it.

To prevent from drowning you can stand in places where there’s bubbles coming out and wait until you hear a chime and a big bubble will appear. Picking up the bubble restores your breath timer, but you won't be in ball form, so if you were jumping and then land on an enemy you will get hit. Always pick up a bubble before you keep going through the stage

You can use the i-frames to make it past enemies or rough parts of the stages.

On green hill zone, there are monitors hidden on palm trees. If the palm trees are behind you that means there’s a monitor there. You can see the tip of the springs if you look closely.

REGIONAL DIFFERENCES

JP and US version runs at 60fps. However, the EU version runs at 50fps, which makes the game slower. Another way to tell the versions apart is that in the JP/US version, the clouds are moving in stage 1.

There is a bug in all versions where if you touch a spike after losing your rings, you will die immediately, ignoring the i-frames.

There is score in certain parts close to the goal. To get it you have to jump on the spot where they are hidden. I show the spots in MAPS.

If you play the JAPANESE VERSION in a JAPANESE CONSOLE, you will get a 1UP if you score 50.000 points after crossing the goal. Score is useless in the rest of the versions. If you are interested in knowing how the score system works, you can check it out Score system

STAGE SELECT

There is a stage select included in the game as a cheat. You can select any stage, including acts and chaos emerald stages. There are two codes. You have to introduce one of them on the title screen depending on the region

Introduce one, and if it doesn't work, introduce the other one. You will hear a chime before pressing A+START if it worked.

There are other codes like debug mode etc. Check them at S1CODES

CHEATING EMERALDS

If you want to cheat and collect all the chaos emeralds, you can access the bonus stages in the stage select.

Pick SPECIAL STAGE on the stage select menu and collect the gem. Once you collect it, do a soft reset. Introduce the stage select code and pic SPECIAL STAGE again. All gems you collect will be saved. Repeat this over and over until you collect all 6 chaos emeralds.

LIVES

You can see your live count at the bottom left corner of the screen. You start with 3 lifes and 0 continues. When your life count is at 1 means you have no lives left. You can get more lifes collecting 100 rings in each stage or in the bonus level. You can get as many 1UPS as the rings per screen allows (both in all stages and labyrinth stages. You can also find monitors all over the stages that contain 1UP.

CONTINUES

The only way to get continues

is collecting 50 rings in the bonus stage. You will hear a chime once you collect 50 rings so you know you have gotten a continue. You can get only one continue per bonus stage.

You can only see your remaining continues each time you game over. You have as many continues as sonic figures shown on the continue screen. If you have 2 sonics on screen, it means you have 3 continues.

GET INFINITE LIVES

There is a way to easily farm lives. In green hill zone - act 3.

Play at the bottom of the map. You will end up under the waterfall. Collect the 1UP. Next, jump on the yellow spring and walk back on the wooden spiky bridge to get to the other 1UP monitor that stands on a loop platform.

To reach the monitor on the loop, run into the red spring, don’t press any buttons and press jump when you reach the yellow flower in the background. Collect these along 100 rings to get 3 lives in total. Then die and repeat the process as many times as you want.

Hold left right from the start, you will automatically get pushed down. Once you land on the UP, make a jump to press the R. Then let sonic slide onto the next left R and hold left. You want to avoid pressing any R from now on, so tap left sometimes and keep pressing left until you land on the prisms. Keep holding left so you break a few, and once gravity kicks you out, land on the middle 3 candy blocks and just stand there, don’t press any buttons. You will fall on the prisms. Once you fall on them, keep holding left and you will get the emerald.

Once sonic starts falling from gravity, tap right for just a moment (this will avoid the bumper and the rotation) Then don’t press anything and you will land on the prisms. Once you are there, try to get in between the prisms and the bounce.

Avoid pressing the rotation and make it to the left side. Keep pressing left and you will be able to sit on the candy blocks. As soon as you have a chance, jump and stand on the prisms pressing directions until you break them all.

BASIC GENERAL TIPS!

Sonic spins faster if you press any direction, so you will get pushed much faster and if you jump, you will reach more far. It's better to press directions after jumping instead of pressing directions while standing still and then jumping.

In most of the special levels, the best you can do is absolutely nothing. If you make mistakes and press a wrong rotation, try to inmediately jump again to it and rotate the other way quickly to regain control of the maze.
